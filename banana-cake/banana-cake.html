Banana Cake

Banana Cake is a healthy and tasty option for a quick snack or appetizer. It is made with natural and nutritious ingredients, such as shredded chicken, carrots, mayonnaise and seasonings. In addition to being easy to prepare, it can be served on toast, bread and natural snacks.

Ingredients

  • 1 Egg
  • 1 Banana
  • 1 Spoon of Oil
  • 4 Spoons of Milk
  • 4 Spoons of wheat flour
  • 1 Spoons of baking powder
  • 3 Spoons of Sugar (optional)

Steps:

  1. Mash the banana until it becomes a paste and mix with the other ingredients.
  2. Place all ingredients in a mug of approximately 300 ml or more.
  3. Stir until the dough is homogeneous, place in the microwave for 3 minutes, enjoy.
