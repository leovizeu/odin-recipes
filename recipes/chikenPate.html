Chicken Pate

Chicken Pate is so Yummy! Come and see the list to it's recipe.

  • Shredded chicken
  • Maionese
  • Chopped Carrot
  • Corn

Steps:

  1. Cook the chicken.
  2. Shake the pan to separate the chicken.
  3. Shred the chicken by hand.
  4. Chopp the Carrot.
  5. Put all ingredients in a bowl.
  6. Put coriander (optional)
  7. Mix them up.
