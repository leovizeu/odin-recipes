Chicken pâté

Chicken pâté is a healthy and tasty option for a quick snack or appetizer. It is made with natural and nutritious ingredients, such as shredded chicken, carrots, mayonnaise and seasonings. In addition to being easy to prepare, it can be served on toast, bread and natural snacks.

Ingredients

  • 1 Chicken chest
  • 250g of Maionese
  • 1 Carrot
  • 1 can of Corn
  • Coriander (optional)

Steps:

  1. Cook the chicken.
  2. Shake the pan to separate the chicken.
  3. Shred the chicken by hand and discart all bones.
  4. Chopp the Carrot into little pieces.
  5. Put all ingredients in a bowl.
  6. Put coriander (optional)
  7. Mix them up.
  8. Sugestion: Serve with toast or use as a filling in your natural sandwich. enjoy.
